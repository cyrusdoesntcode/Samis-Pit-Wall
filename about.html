Why I Built This

I'm a Red Bull Racing fan, and this page is my own space to write about the team I follow every race weekend. It's also my web development assignment: I picked a free template, stripped out all of the original placeholder content and images, and rebuilt it around something I actually care about.

The Team

Oracle Red Bull Racing is based in Milton Keynes, England, and is currently racing its 21st season in Formula 1. Since entering as a full works team, they've built a reputation as one of the sport's most successful outfits, with multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships to their name. The Max Verstappen era has been the team's most dominant stretch yet, with four World titles secured so far.

2026: A New Era

Formula 1 introduced its biggest technical regulation shake-up in years for 2026, with all-new chassis and power unit rules across the grid. Red Bull Racing is tackling the change with its own Ford-backed power unit for the first time, replacing the previous Honda supply. On the driver side, Isack Hadjar was promoted to partner Max Verstappen after an impressive rookie season with the team's sister squad, with Yuki Tsunoda moving into a reserve and test driver role.
